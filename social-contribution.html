Social Contribution

Small contributions add up to big impact

We here at Grit77 also work towards the social contribution with the help of an NGO, Shaurya - Beyond Barriers..

Shaurya Foundation Trust started is a centre for neurodiverse adults, that concentrates not just on educating people with special adults, but also enabling them to be self-sufficient and earn a livelihood. The organization believes in upskilling, employing, and creating employment opportunities for them.

Grit77 works directly with the NGO and feels immense happiness to provide our contribution.

With the help of Shaurya Foundation Trust, we promote the products which are made by the children from the NGO and encourage the corporate bodies to inculcate such products in their corporate gifting kits.

Grit77 is working towards a new culture of corporate needs. Grit77 aims at developing the first of a kind experience when it comes to branding
